Range modes overview

Statistical (CLT) shows a confidence interval for outcomes if you could repeat the purchase many times. It narrows as you buy more boxes (~1/√n).

Structural (model band) shows uncertainty from slot‑odds bounds (≤ or ranges). It scales roughly linearly with boxes; it’s not a statistical interval.

Play Boosters = 30 packs/box Probabilities normalized to 14 Magic cards/pack (tokens not scaled) Sift defaults to ≥ $0.25

Confidence levels (for Statistical mode)

For a given purchase, a C% band would contain your realized value about C% of the time under the model. Higher confidence ⇒ wider band.

How to use

  1. Open one of the tools above.
  2. Click Build list to fetch live Scryfall data and normalize odds.
  3. Set boxes to buy, choose Statistical or Structural range, and (optionally) adjust the Sift threshold.
  4. Read EV across your purchase and per 30‑pack box (Low / Est. / High).
